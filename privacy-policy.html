MealHub Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how MealHub ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App") and services. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, password, profile photo.
  • Location data: precise (GPS) and approximate location to show nearby restaurants, calculate delivery distance, and route drivers in real time.
  • Camera and photos: we access the device camera and photo library only when you choose to upload a profile picture, food image, or delivery proof.
  • Microphone: used only during in-app voice calls between customer, driver, and restaurant.
  • Payment information: processed securely by Stripe. We do not store full card numbers on our servers.
  • Order and usage data: order history, in-app interactions, device identifiers, app version, crash logs.
  • Notifications: push tokens to deliver order, call, and delivery status alerts.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide food ordering, delivery tracking, and account services.
  • Match drivers with orders and route deliveries.
  • Process payments and prevent fraud.
  • Send transactional notifications (orders, deliveries, calls).
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Sharing of Information

We share information only as needed to operate the service:

  • Restaurants and drivers: name, order details, and delivery location to fulfill your order.
  • Payment processor (Stripe): to charge and refund payments.
  • Cloud providers (Supabase, Google Firebase, Agora): to host data, send notifications, and power voice calls.
  • Legal authorities: when required by law.

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Permissions

  • Camera: capture profile photos and delivery proof images. Optional.
  • Photos / Media: select images from your gallery. Optional.
  • Location (foreground & background): show nearby restaurants and track active deliveries. Drivers require background location to share live position with customers during a delivery.
  • Microphone: in-app voice calls between participants of an order. Optional.
  • Notifications: deliver order, call, and delivery updates.

5. Data Retention

We retain account and order data while your account is active and for as long as needed to comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 8).

6. Security

We use TLS encryption in transit, encrypted storage at rest, role-based access controls, and Row-Level Security on our databases. No system is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children

MealHub is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Access, correct, or delete your account data from in-app Settings.
  • Revoke camera, location, microphone, or notification permissions in your device settings.
  • Email us to request a copy or deletion of all your data.

9. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in jurisdictions outside your country of residence. We use providers that comply with recognized data protection frameworks.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent revision.
